Partnering with Action Model allows you to leverage the power of Large Action Models (LAMs) to provide cutting-edge automation solutions to your clients. By becoming a reseller, you can build a business around automating complex digital workflows, offering high-value services to companies of all sizes.

## Why Partner with Us?

- **Access Powerful Technology**: Utilize our state-of-the-art LAMs to tackle automation challenges that are impossible to solve with traditional tools.
- **Automate Anything**: Go beyond APIs and automate any graphical user interface (GUI) on web or desktop applications.
- **Create New Revenue Streams**: Offer automation services, sell pre-built workflow templates, or integrate Action Model into your existing products.
- **Full Support & Training**: We provide our partners with the training, resources, and dedicated support needed to succeed.

## Who is this for?

- **Consulting Firms & Agencies**: Expand your service offerings with powerful AI automation capabilities.

- **Entrepreneurs**: Build a new business focused on the rapidly growing AI automation market.
